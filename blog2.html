Your First Million — 7 Days

The game never tells you the optimal money sequence. It throws you into Vice City with $500 and a vague suggestion to "complete missions." That's the slow road. Here's the fast one.

This guide assumes you're starting fresh at Level 1 with the tutorial complete. Everything here is achievable on your first playthrough, no prior knowledge needed. Follow the day-by-day plan and you'll hit $1,000,000 by Day 7 — often faster.

Before you start

Do NOT spend money on clothes, cars or weapons in your first 48 hours. Every dollar counts in the early game. The only exceptions are noted in the guide below.

Day 1 — The foundation ($42,000)

01
Story missions + ATM farming
Target earnings: $42,000
  1. Complete the first 4 story missions without skipping cutscenes. The game forces you to watch them once and they contain intel about upcoming opportunities. You'll earn $12,000–$18,000 for these four missions combined.
  2. After mission 4, you'll have a vehicle and basic weapons. Head to the Brickell financial district. Identify the three ATMs on your minimap — they're marked as bank icons.
  3. Watch NPCs using the ATM. The moment one finishes, approach them and mug them. Business district NPCs carry $200–$800 each. In 45 minutes you can cycle through all three ATMs repeatedly for $8,000–$14,000.
  4. Sell your stolen vehicle at the Los Santos Customs south of Brickell ($4,200 for anything you pick up off the street in this district).
Efficiency note

Do ATM runs between 6pm–10pm in-game time. Business district foot traffic is highest then — more NPCs withdrawing, more targets per hour.

Day 1 total~$42,000

Day 2 — First business ($89,000)

02
Repo missions + unlock your first business
Target earnings: $89,000
  1. Simeon Yetarian repo missions are available from Day 1 on the in-game phone. Each repo pays $12,000–$18,000 and takes 8–12 minutes. Do 4 in a row — $48,000–$72,000 before lunch.
  2. With $90,000+ in your account, buy the Vespucci Canals warehouse ($75,000). This is your first passive income property. It generates $3,200 per in-game day without you doing anything.
  3. Do two more story missions in the evening. Combined with the repo runs you should clear $89,000 net after the warehouse purchase.
Why this property

The Vespucci warehouse is the only sub-$80K property in GTA 6 with a positive daily return. Every other property at this price point costs more to maintain than it earns.

Day 2 total~$89,000

Days 3–4 — Stack and invest ($280,000)

By Day 3 you've unlocked Martin Madrazo contact missions from completing a specific story chapter. These pay $20,000–$35,000 each and are the best contact missions in the early game by far. Run four per day.

03–04
Madrazo missions + ViceCoin entry
Target earnings: $280,000
  1. Run Madrazo missions in the morning session — 4 missions, ~$110,000 across both days.
  2. On Day 3 evening, check ViceCoin.net on your in-game browser. If VCoin is below $0.40 (it usually is at this point in a fresh game's timeline), buy $40,000 worth. Set a reminder to check it again on Day 6.
  3. Continue building your warehouse passive income — resupply it once on Day 4 ($3,500 cost, unlocks $18,000 in product).
  4. Complete 2 story missions each day. You should be approaching the heist unlock missions which massively increase your per-session earnings.
Days 3–4 total~$280,000

Day 5 — First heist ($218,000)

05
Cargo Ship heist (first run) + passive harvest
Target earnings: $218,000
  1. By Day 5 you should be Level 15+ from story and contact missions. The Cargo Ship heist is now available. Run it solo on Normal difficulty for your first attempt — takes 50 minutes, pays $1.8M base but your cut at this stage (before crew synergy) is approximately $180,000 solo.
  2. Collect the passive income from your warehouse — $38,000 has accumulated over 3 days.
Don't rush the heist

Day 5 is the earliest you'll be ready. Running it at Level 10–12 means you get the lower payout tier. Wait for Level 15 — the difference is $40,000 per run.

Day 5 total~$218,000

Days 6–7 — The push to $1M ($471,000)

06–07
ViceCoin exit + second business + second heist
Target earnings: $471,000
  1. Day 6 morning: check ViceCoin. If you bought at $0.40 and it's now $0.65+ (typical 3-day movement), sell your entire position — $65,000 return on $40,000 investment in 72 hours.
  2. Purchase the Brickell district nightclub ($120,000). This is your second passive income stream — $8,500 per in-game day and it stacks with the warehouse.
  3. Run 3 more Madrazo missions on Day 6 while your businesses accumulate.
  4. Day 7: run the Cargo Ship heist again — you now have better gear and higher level, pushing your cut to $220,000+. Combined with two days of passive income from both businesses ($23,400), you cross $1 million.
Days 6–7 total~$471,000

What not to do

Avoid these traps

Don't buy a luxury apartment in Week 1. Don't buy a supercar. Don't spend money on custom weapons until Day 5 minimum. These feel good and kill your momentum. The players who hit $1M fastest are the ones who delay all vanity spending until after the milestone.
