GTA 6 Confirmed Vehicle List So Far

Rockstar games are defined by their driving mechanics and vehicle catalogs. Based on frame-by-frame analysis of the official trailers and leaked developer builds, here is the complete catalog of confirmed vehicles.

From lowriders on Vice Beach to airboats in the Everglades, the transportation options in GTA 6 represent the diverse car culture of the American Southeast. Let's break down the list by vehicle category.

The ultimate GTA 6 confirmed vehicle list so far

Our editorial team analyzed every single frame of the trailer releases and cross-referenced them with in-game asset names found in developer leaks. We've verified over 80 vehicles, ranging from returning fan-favorites to brand-new models making their series debut. Handling mechanics have also been upgraded, featuring realistic tire deformation, suspension loading, and vehicle-specific dashboard cluster displays.

Supercars and High-Performance Sports Cars

Leonida's elite crowd loves to show off high-end imports. The following top-tier speed machines have been spotted:

  • Grotti Cheetah Classic: The iconic 80s supercar returns to the neon streets of Vice Beach where it belongs.
  • Pegassi Zorrusso: A modern hypercar built for cutting through city traffic at extreme velocities.
  • Pfister Comet S2 Cabrio: A premium German sports coupe, seen cruising down Ocean Drive.
  • Grotti Furia: A sleek hypercar boasting extreme downforce and acceleration ratings.
Customization tip

Handling specs suggest that high-performance supercars suffer massive traction losses when driven on unpaved dirt roads in the Everglades. Stick to the highway or swap to an off-road class when heading inland.

Street Tuners and Muscle Cars

Leonida's underground car meets are dominated by custom modifications and muscle. Verified models include:

  • Bravado Gauntlet Hellfire: The loudest, most aggressive muscle car in the game, perfect for high-speed straight-line pursuits.
  • Imponte Ruiner: A classic muscle coupe making its return with massive custom spoiler options.
  • Karin Futo GTX: The favorite drift machine of the imports crowd, spotted at Port Gellhorn parking lot meets.
  • Vapid Dominator ASP: A high-revving muscle car that offers a great balance of acceleration and power slide control.

Watercraft and Aviation

With massive lakes, channels, and bays, aquatic vehicles are a core gameplay element:

  • Sea Sparrow: A light amphibious helicopter equipped with pontoons for landing on waterways.
  • Nagasaki Dinghy: The reliable military-grade inflatable boat, frequently used as a getaway vehicle for water-based heists.
  • Everglades Airboat: A flat-bottomed boat powered by an aircraft engine, designed specifically for gliding over mud banks and swamp grass.
  • Shitzu Tropic: A luxury motor yacht, spotted anchored off the keys with detailed interior cabins.
