Server rules

Keep it
clean

  • No unverified info. Post cheat codes, coordinates or strategies with proof. Unverified claims get removed. Three removals = ban.
  • No spoilers without tags. Use the [SPOILER] tag or Discord spoiler formatting for story content. People play at different paces.
  • No cheating other members. Scamming crew members, stealing cargo, or deliberately ruining heist runs = permanent ban, no appeal.
  • No self-promotion. This isn't a place to plug your channel, site, or service. Vice Vault content only.
  • Be useful or be quiet. Low-effort posts, spam, and "gg" chains in announcement threads get deleted. Keep discussion signal-to-noise high.
  • English only in main channels. We can't moderate what we can't read. Regional language channels may be added if there's demand.
