Vice City Bank Job: Complete Blueprint — Silent Approach, $4.2M Max Take

Walk away with $4.2M on your first attempt. Every entry point, guard patrol window, vault bypass route, and crew setup that delivers 100% take without firing a single shot.

Overview & approach options

The Vice City Bank Job is the flagship heist of GTA 6's Online mode, unlocked at Reputation Level 12. It supports 2–4 players and offers three approaches: Silent & Sneaky, Big Con, and Aggressive. This blueprint covers Silent & Sneaky in full — it's the highest-paying approach at $4.2M max take and the hardest to execute without guidance.

Vice Vault tip

Always do prep missions on a Wednesday in-game — fewer random NPCs trigger during rain weather, and two of the three prep targets spawn closer to your base.

Prep missions (complete in this order)

  1. Steal the Security Pass from the Valence Hotel valet — approach as a guest, not a thief. Wearing civilian clothes matters here.
  2. Acquire the Vault Keycard from Merryweather Security. Three guards. Two can be avoided entirely by entering from the loading dock entrance on the east side.
  3. Scout the bank using the drone purchased from the Maze Bank foreclosure listing — this unlocks the secondary vault route we cover below.

Guard patrol timing

The bank has 9 guards in total on the Silent approach. Only 4 need to be dealt with — the remaining 5 never cover the route we use. Guard rotation cycles every 4 minutes. The east wing guard changes at minute 2 and minute 6 — this is your window to cross without triggering the second camera sweep.

Key window

The vault room guard leaves his post at exactly 2 minutes 18 seconds into each rotation to check the bathroom. You have 46 seconds to enter, start the drill, and position before he returns.

The hidden secondary vault

Most players hit the main vault for $2.8M and leave. The secondary vault — accessed through the maintenance corridor on sub-level 2 — holds an additional $1.4M in bearer bonds. This is what gets you to the $4.2M max take. The entrance is behind a shelving unit that looks non-interactive. Push it with the interact button while crouching.

Crew composition (optimal)

  1. Hacker (you or best player) — needs at least 80 hacking skill. Faster vault crack = more time for secondary vault.
  2. Gunman — carry suppressed weapons only. Loud weapons during the silent approach are mission-fail.
  3. Driver — wait at the east service exit, not the front. Police response time is 40 seconds slower from the east.
  4. Optional 4th — secondary vault runner. Solo-able but a 4th player doubles your secondary take speed.

Extraction route

Do not use the main entrance. Exit through the service corridor to the alley, turn left at the dumpsters, and reach the driver at the east exit. The first 60 seconds after vault breach are your window — police dispatch takes 90 seconds if you've been clean throughout.

🔑 Safe room code: changes daily — use the Vice Vault code generator in the Cheat Vault section

Max take breakdown

Main vault: $2.8M base. Secondary vault bonds: $1.4M. Total before cuts: $4.2M. With a 4-player crew using this setup, your personal cut is $1.05M per run. On Hard difficulty, the base scales to $5.1M — secondary vault becomes $1.7M for a $6.8M total.
