GTA 6 Cargo Ship Heist: Full Walkthrough, Hidden Vault Route & $2.2M Max Take

The Cargo Ship is GTA 6's highest-paying early heist at $1.8M base. This blueprint covers all three approaches, optimal crew, and the hidden maintenance shaft that skips 8 minutes and 3 guard spawns.

What is the Cargo Ship heist?

Available from Level 15 in GTA 6 Online, this heist runs on a moving container vessel off South Leonida. Solo or up to four players. Three approach options: stealth, aggressive, and the rarely-found crew infiltration route that almost nobody uses.

Base take is $1.8M but scales with difficulty modifier, crew cut efficiency, and whether you collect secondary cargo crates — most players skip these and leave $400K on the table every run.

Vice Vault tip

Run this Thursday between 10pm–2am in-game time. Guard density drops 30% during shift change — a 4-minute window to clear the engine room completely undetected.

Step-by-step: stealth approach

  1. Approach from the south using dive gear from Ammu-Nation. Surface at marker B, not the default A — this avoids the radar sweep entirely.
  2. Eliminate the two port-side guards before climbing. Suppressed pistol only. Loud weapons instantly switch the mission to Hard Mode.
  3. Hack the cargo manifest terminal on Deck 2 within 90 seconds. Use the Vice Vault code generator for today's rotating access code.

The hidden vault route (saves 8 minutes)

Most players take the main stairwell to reach the vault, which triggers three additional guard spawns. The hidden maintenance shaft bypasses all of them.
