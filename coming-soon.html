FAQ

When does Vice Vault launch? +

Vice Vault launches the same day GTA 6 releases publicly. Waitlist members get access 24 hours before the public launch. We're building content and infrastructure now so the vault is fully stocked on day one.

Is the waitlist actually free? +

Yes, completely free. No credit card, no commitment. Joining the waitlist gives you early access on launch day and locks in launch-day pricing. There's no obligation to pay anything.

What platforms does Vice Vault cover? +

All platforms GTA 6 releases on — PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S at launch, with PC guides added once the PC version releases. Cheat codes are provided for all platforms separately.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled any time with no penalty and no questions asked. Your access remains until the end of your billing period. Annual Elite Crew members who cancel within 30 days of subscribing receive a full refund.

Is Vice Vault affiliated with Rockstar Games? +

No. Vice Vault is a fully independent gaming guide site — not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Rockstar Games or Take-Two Interactive in any way. All guides are original editorial content, similar to IGN, GameFAQs, and GameRant. Game guides and cheat codes are not protected by copyright.

How is Vice Vault different from free guides on YouTube? +

YouTube guides are made for views, not depth. They skip steps, bury the best info at the end, and are never updated when the meta changes. Vice Vault guides are written documents you can follow step-by-step, with hidden routes, exact numbers, and weekly updates. No ads mid-sentence. No 10-minute preamble.
