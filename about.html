How we operate

The Vice Vault
rules

01
Test before publish

Every guide is play-tested before it goes live. If we haven't done it ourselves or had a community member verify it with video, it doesn't go in the vault. No exceptions.

02
Update when it changes

Rockstar patches things. The meta shifts. A route that earned $800K/hr last week might earn $200K this week. We track changes and update guides within 24 hours of a patch.

03
Credit the community

Some of our best discoveries come from community members. When a member finds something, we verify it, publish it, and credit them by name. The vault belongs to all of us.

04
No padding, ever

We don't write 3,000-word guides to hit an SEO word count. If a guide can be done in 400 words, it's 400 words. Your time is more valuable than our traffic metrics.

05
Stay legal

We publish game guides, walkthroughs and cheat codes — all of which are legally protected as editorial content. We use zero Rockstar assets, logos or trademarked imagery. We're fans, not freeloaders.

06
Members first

Paying members get every weekly drop 48 hours before the public. They get custom blueprint requests. They get the Discord. They get priority. That's the deal, and we honour it every week.
