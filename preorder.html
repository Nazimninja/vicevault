No. Pre-ordering is free. You give us your email and lock in your price. We send you a checkout link on launch day when Vice Vault goes live. You only pay then — and your card is never charged automatically without your action.

What does "price locked forever" actually mean?+

If you pre-order Vault Pro at ₹499/mo, you pay ₹499 every month for as long as you're subscribed — even if we raise the price to ₹699 or ₹899 in the future. Your rate is frozen at ₹499 permanently, documented in your account at signup.

What if GTA 6 gets delayed?+

Your pre-order lock stays valid regardless of GTA 6's release date. We launch Vice Vault the same day GTA 6 becomes publicly available. If GTA 6 is delayed, Vice Vault is delayed too — and your price lock extends with it. You're never charged until launch day.

Can I cancel after subscribing?+

Yes, anytime. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled from your dashboard with no penalty. You keep access until the end of your billing period. Elite Crew subscribers get a full refund if they cancel within 30 days of their first payment.

What is the Founding Member badge?+

The Founding Member badge is a permanent Discord and profile badge for Elite Crew pre-orders only. It marks you as someone who was there before launch. It is not available after GTA 6 launches — not for any price. This is the only way to get it.

How is payment processed?+

Payments are processed by Razorpay (India's leading payment platform). We accept UPI, all major debit/credit cards, net banking, and wallets including GPay, PhonePe, Paytm and Mobikwik. We never see or store your card details.
